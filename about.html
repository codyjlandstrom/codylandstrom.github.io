CODY LANDSTROM

About Cody

THE SHOW

Cody combines the skills of comedy magic, astonishing sleight of hand and masterful juggling into a custom tailored performance. Interactive and fun, Cody can customize his award-winning act or create new pieces from your group right into the act adding to the fun for everyone.

See the Cups and Balls, Incredible Rope Tricks, Objects vanish before your eyes, The Teleporting Silks, Meet Gus the Mouse, Expert Lassoing, Amazing Juggling, Stunning Mind Reading, Balancing Stunts, A trick from Ancient Egypt, Impossible Feats of Dexterity, and more.

Cody’s performance is suitable for any size, any occasion, whether it’s a small intimate party, a large corporate trade show, cocktail hour walk-around or an after banquet show, experience a memorable time, filled with laughter, mystery, fun and audience participation.

CODY'S STORY

Cody Landstrom has an extensive background as an entertainer. A seasoned professional, Cody has performed as a professional musician for over 17 years throughout the US, Canada and Europe. In every imaginable venue, including several years with USO tours.

In 1987 he teamed up with Eric Mead and the Great Loudini as the "Dynamo Masters", a high-tech magic, juggling, magic and comedy show that performed all over the U.S. Cody now presents custom tailored performances for corporations, celebrities, and others, leaving them laughing and mystified.
